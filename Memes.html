Minecraft FAQs

How do I tame a horse?

To tame a horse, mount it repeatedly until hearts appear, indicating that it is tamed.

What are potions used for?

Potions can provide various effects, such as healing, strength, or speed, and are created using a brewing stand.

How can I find villages?

Villages can be found in plains, savannas, and desert biomes, usually located near water sources.
