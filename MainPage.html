Minecraft FAQs

How can I create a shelter?

To create a shelter, gather blocks like wood or stone and place them to form walls and a roof.

What are Minecraft mobs?

Mobs are living creatures in Minecraft, including animals like cows and pigs, as well as hostile creatures like zombies and skeletons.

How do I enchant items?

You can enchant items by using an enchantment table, which requires experience points and lapis lazuli.
