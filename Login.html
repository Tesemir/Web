Minecraft FAQs

How do I build a farm?

To build a farm, clear a flat area, till the soil with a hoe, and plant seeds. Make sure to have a water source nearby for irrigation.

What are enchantments?

Enchantments are special bonuses that can be applied to tools, weapons, and armor to enhance their abilities.

How do I use a furnace?

A furnace is used to smelt ores, cook food, and create various items. Place fuel in the bottom slot and the item to be smelted in the top slot.
