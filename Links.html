Minecraft FAQs

What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox game where players can build and explore an open world.

How do you gather resources?

To gather resources, use tools such as a pickaxe, axe, or shovel to collect blocks.

What game modes are available?

Minecraft offers several game modes, including Creative Mode, Survival Mode, and Hardcore Mode.
